CS with Gaming

This emphasis of computer science focuses on art with computer science alongside a thorough understanding of computer science principles. This is so that individuals who pursue this degree have a strong foundation in computer science so that they are qualitfied for any computer science job.



Some objectives of this emphasis is:

- Be able to demonstrate understanding of and ability to apply core Computer Science principles, knowledge and skills to solve problems using computers.

- Be able to demonstrate understanding of and ability to apply gaming and animation knowledge and skills to solve problems in the computer gaming industry.

- Be prepared for a career and/or vocation using Computer Science, especially in the gaming and animation field.

- Be introduced to gaming engines and learn how to use them.
