Which Flavor of Computer Science?

You may have guessed, but computer science is actually a rather vague term. Computer science for the most part refers to just about any job that utilizes code to perform important functions on one or more computers. This can apply to several different jobs, anywhere from hundreds to even thousands of jobs, which may fall under computer science, but can be completely different in many ways. With this in mind, it can be very important to get an idea of what you want from your career in computer science, which can narrow down which field you'd like to go into.

Computer Science as a Career

If your specialty is just computer science as a whole, your options for careers will most likely be very flexible, ranging from web development to software engineering. If you don't have any particular focus with code, regular computer science can be a good route to take to find good jobs without limiting your options too much.
