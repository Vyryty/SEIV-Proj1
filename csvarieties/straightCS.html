Computer Science

Computer science as a basis is a solid degree to take. Today as a result of massive technological advancements, computer scientists are more vital than ever.

Some objectives of this emphasis is:

  • Desire to learn constantly

    • The technology world is rapidly developing, in order to keep up with it and be ahead of the competition you need to be able to constantly learn about the field. Such topics may be about programming languages to hardware.

  • Knowlege of various technologies

    • For all the things we use on a daily basis, you need to have knowledge of the tools you may need to build or maintain technologies.

  • Problem solving mindset

    • A big part of computer science involves solving problems, or planning how to solve a problem. You will most certainly need to solve some sort of problem along the way.
