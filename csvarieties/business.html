Computer Science with business

Computer science with business is a good field because it combines two very profitable careers, Computer Science and Business. This field is for you if you like to combine technical problem-solving with the strategy of business.

You may enjoy Computer Science with Business if you like math, accounting, or general problem-solving using computers. Jobs in Computer Science with Business include titles like an Expert Applications Developer, Business Systems Analyst (BSA) / ERP Administrator, or Business Analyst. Courses you may take in this field include Programming, Management Information Systems, and Macroeconomics.

Computer science with business is a good field of study if you are wanting to answer the question, "How can computers help me do business?"
